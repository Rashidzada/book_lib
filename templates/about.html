Our Story

Hi, I'm Rashid Zada, a Software Engineer. I'm passionate about creating Book Lib, an online platform designed to make accessing all types of books easier for students and teachers. Our goal is to facilitate sharing among educators, making educational resources more accessible to students worldwide.

Our mission, Mission 2030, is to empower everyone through education. We believe that knowledge should be accessible to all, and we strive to make that a reality by providing a diverse range of books and resources for learning.

Additionally, we leverage the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in education. AI is the future, capable of multiplying your educational potential tenfold. By integrating AI technologies into our platform, we aim to personalize learning experiences, optimize content delivery, and foster innovative teaching methods.
